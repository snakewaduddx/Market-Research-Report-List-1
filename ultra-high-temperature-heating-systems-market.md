<p><ul><li>Direct Heating</li><li>Indirect Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra High Temperature (UHT) heating systems can be categorized into direct and indirect heating methods. Direct heating involves applying heat directly to the material, ensuring rapid heating and minimal energy losses, making it suitable for processes requiring quick temperature changes. In contrast, indirect heating transfers heat through a medium, providing more uniform heating and reducing the risk of contamination, making it ideal for sensitive applications. Both types cater to various industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in thermal processing.</p></p>
