<p><p>The Wireless IoT Sensors market can be categorized into two main types: Traditional Wireless Technology and LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Networks) Technology. Traditional Wireless Technology includes protocols like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, offering higher data rates but limited range and battery life. In contrast, LPWANs are designed for long-range communication with low power consumption, making them ideal for applications where devices transmit small amounts of data infrequently. This distinction influences deployment strategies in various sectors, including smart cities and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
