<p><strong>The Automotive Cam Phaser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane-type Cam Phaser</li><li>Star Shaped Cam Phaser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cam phaser market primarily consists of two types: vane-type cam phasers and star-shaped cam phasers. Vane-type cam phasers utilize hydraulic mechanisms with vanes to adjust the timing of the camshaft and optimize engine performance and emissions under varying conditions. In contrast, star-shaped cam phasers employ a simpler design based on a star-shaped mechanism that alters camshaft position through indexed movements, providing a cost-effective solution for timing adjustments in various engine applications.</p></p>
