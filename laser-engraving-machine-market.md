<p><p>The laser engraving machine market consists of several types, each suited for specific applications. CO2 laser engraving machines are ideal for non-metal materials like wood and acrylic, offering high precision. Fiber laser engraving machines excel in marking metal surfaces due to their efficiency and speed. Diode laser engraving machines are compact and suitable for light materials, making them user-friendly. Nd:YAG laser engraving machines, known for their robustness, are effective for deep engraving in metals and hard materials, ideal for industrial applications.</p></p>
