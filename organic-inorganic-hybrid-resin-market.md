<p><ul><li>EB Cured</li><li>UV Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic-inorganic hybrid resin market features two primary types: EB cured and UV cured resins. EB cured resins utilize electron beam technology for cross-linking, providing enhanced mechanical properties and chemical resistance. They are ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, UV cured resins use ultraviolet light for rapid curing, offering quick processing times and flexibility in application. Both types cater to diverse sectors such as automotive, electronics, and coatings, leveraging their unique properties for superior performance and efficiency.</p></p>
