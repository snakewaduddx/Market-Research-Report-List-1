<p><p>The COPD and asthma devices market includes various inhalation delivery systems. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) dispense a specific dose of medication in aerosol form, while Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) release medication in powdered form, requiring patient inhalation to activate. Soft Mist Inhalers create a slow-moving mist for easier inhalation. Jet Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist using air pressure, whereas Ultrasonic Nebulizers use ultrasound vibrations for the same purpose. Other devices may include spacers and nebulization systems that cater to specific needs.</p></p>
