<p><p>Dental Glass Ionomer Fillings are tooth-colored materials that are commonly used in restorative dentistry. They are made from a mixture of acrylic acid and glass powder, allowing them to chemically bond to the tooth structure. These fillings offer advantages such as fluoride release, which can help prevent further decay, and they are less sensitive to moisture during the curing process compared to resin-based materials. Their aesthetic appeal and biocompatibility make them popular for both anterior and posterior restorations.</p><p>The Dental Glass Ionomer Fillings Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for aesthetically pleasing dental solutions and a surge in dental caries cases among various demographics.
