<p><p>The penetrating primer market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component primers are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, providing convenience and ease of application. They typically cure through air drying and are suitable for a variety of substrates. In contrast, two-component primers require the mixing of a base and hardener, resulting in enhanced durability and chemical resistance. This type is ideal for demanding environments where performance is critical. Both types cater to specific application needs and user preferences.</p></p>
