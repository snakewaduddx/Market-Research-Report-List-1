Bipolar demultiplexers provide faster switching times but consume more power, suitable for high-frequency operations. CMOS technology is known for its low power dissipation and scalability, making it favorable for battery-operated devices. Each type caters to specific application needs across telecommunications, consumer electronics, and computing sectors.</p></p>
