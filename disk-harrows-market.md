<p><p>The disk harrow market is segmented into three main types: single action, offset type, and double action. Single action harrows utilize a single set of disks, providing efficient soil tillage for compact areas. Offset type harrows are designed to operate offset from the tractor, allowing for better coverage and efficiency when navigating larger fields. Double action harrows feature two sets of disks working in tandem, enhancing soil mixing and breaking up clumps more effectively, making them ideal for diverse soil conditions.</p></p>
