<p><p>Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) utilizes mirrors or lenses to concentrate sunlight, generating heat that drives steam turbines for electricity production. Key market types include Trench Concentrating Solar Power Systems, characterized by linear troughs that focus sunlight onto receivers; Tower-type Solar Power Tower Systems, which use a central tower surrounded by mirrors to capture and concentrate solar energy; and Dish Concentrating Solar Power Systems, featuring parabolic dishes that direct sunlight to a receiver. Other markets may involve innovative hybrid systems or enhanced storage solutions.</p></p>
