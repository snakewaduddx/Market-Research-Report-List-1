<p><p>The Mycobacterium tuberculosis testing market encompasses several key types. Point of Care Tests (POCT) provide rapid results at the patient's location, enhancing immediate decision-making. Nucleic Acid Amplification Tests (NAAT) offer high sensitivity and specificity by directly detecting the genetic material of the pathogen.
