<p><p>The Styreneic Block Copolymers (SBC) market is primarily divided into three types: SEBS, SIS, and other variants. SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene) offers excellent elasticity and UV resistance, making it suitable for automotive and roofing applications. SIS (styrene-isoprene-styrene) is known for its flexibility and adhesion properties, commonly used in adhesives and sealants. Other variants encompass specialized copolymers tailored for niche applications in industries like packaging, toys, and medical devices, highlighting the versatility of SBCs across various sectors.</p></p>
