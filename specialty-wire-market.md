<p><ul><li>Copper Clad Wire</li><li>Electroplated Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty wire market encompasses various types, notably copper clad wire and electroplated wire. Copper clad wire consists of a core of a less expensive metal coated with a layer of copper, providing cost-effectiveness and good conductivity. Electroplated wire, on the other hand, is coated with a metal layer through an electrochemical process, enhancing properties like corrosion resistance and conductivity. Both types cater to industries requiring specific electrical and thermal performance, making them vital in applications such as electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
