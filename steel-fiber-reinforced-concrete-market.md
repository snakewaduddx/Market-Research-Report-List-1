<p><ul><li>Cut Wire (Cold Drawn) Steel Fiber Type</li><li>Slit Sheet Steel Fiber Type</li><li>Melt Extract Steel Fiber Type</li><li>Mill Cut Steel Fiber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete market comprises various types of steel fibers, each offering distinct benefits for enhancing concrete performance. Cut Wire (Cold Drawn) Steel Fiber Type features straight cut wires that improve tensile strength and crack resistance. Slit Sheet Steel Fiber Type consists of thin, slit sheets that provide excellent distribution and flexibility. Melt Extract Steel Fiber Type involves fibers derived from molten metal, offering high durability. Mill Cut Steel Fiber Type uses cut strands from rolled steel, optimizing concrete toughness and performance.</p></p>
