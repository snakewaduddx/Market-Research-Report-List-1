<p><strong>The Cosmetic Laminated Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic laminated tube packaging mainly comprises two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes are made from an aluminum layer sandwiched between plastic layers, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture and UV light, making them suitable for high-end cosmetic products. Conversely, PBL tubes are solely composed of plastics, providing flexibility and being lightweight, making them ideal for a wide range of personal care items. Both types cater to different needs in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
