<p><p>The photovoltaic combiner box market is divided into two main types: high current and low current. High current combiner boxes are designed to handle higher amperage, often used in larger solar installations where multiple photovoltaic strings need to be connected efficiently while minimizing energy loss. Conversely, low current combiner boxes are suited for smaller systems, managing lower amperage, making them ideal for residential or small commercial applications. Both types enhance safety and simplify the wiring process in solar energy systems.</p></p>
