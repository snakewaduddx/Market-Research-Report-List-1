<p><p>The titanium powder market primarily comprises two types: High Purity Titanium Powder (CPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP). CPTP is characterized by a minimal presence of impurities, making it ideal for applications in aerospace and biomedical fields where material integrity is critical. In contrast, ATP includes various alloy compositions, incorporating elements like aluminum and vanadium, enhancing properties such as strength and corrosion resistance. These powders cater to diverse industries, including additive manufacturing, automotive, and chemical processing.</p></p>
