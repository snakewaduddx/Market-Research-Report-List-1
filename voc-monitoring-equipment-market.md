<p><strong>The VOC Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) monitoring equipment is vital for assessing air quality and environmental safety. Two prominent types are Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gases, providing rapid and accurate measurements of a wide range of VOCs at low concentrations. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in electrical conductivity within a semiconductor material when exposed to specific gases, offering a cost-effective solution for various applications. Both types are essential for environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
