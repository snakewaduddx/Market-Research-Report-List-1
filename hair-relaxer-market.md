<p><p>The hair relaxer market is primarily divided into two types: lye relaxers and no-lye relaxers. Lye relaxers contain sodium hydroxide, effectively straightening hair quickly but may cause irritation for some users. In contrast, no-lye relaxers use alternative chemicals like calcium hydroxide and are milder, making them suitable for sensitive scalps. Both types cater to varied consumer preferences, with lye relaxers often favored for their potency and no-lye options appealing to those seeking gentler formulations.</p></p>
