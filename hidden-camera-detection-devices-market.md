<p><ul><li>RF Based</li><li>LED Light Gauge Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hidden camera detection devices are critical for privacy protection and security. RF-based devices identify signals emitted by wireless cameras by scanning radio frequencies, effectively locating hidden surveillance equipment. In contrast, LED light gauge-based detectors utilize specialized light to reveal lens reflections from cameras, making them visible to the naked eye. Both technologies serve distinct purposes in the market, offering users reliable methods to ensure their environments remain private and secure from unauthorized monitoring.</p></p>
