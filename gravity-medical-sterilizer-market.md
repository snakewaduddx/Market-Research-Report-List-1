<p><p>Gravity medical sterilizers are devices that utilize gravity displacement methods to sterilize medical instruments and equipment using steam. This process involves the introduction of steam into the sterilization chamber, where heavier air is displaced, ensuring thorough penetration and effective sterilization. Gravity sterilizers are widely used in healthcare facilities, including hospitals and surgical centers, due to their reliability and ease of operation.</p><p>The Gravity Medical Sterilizer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing demand for effective sterilization solutions in the healthcare sector.
