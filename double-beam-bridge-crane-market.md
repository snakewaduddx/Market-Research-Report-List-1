<p><p>The double beam bridge crane market includes various types such as gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes have a complete frame that supports the hoisting system, allowing for movement in large outdoor areas or warehouses. They are versatile and commonly used in construction and shipping. Half-gantry cranes, on the other hand, have one side supported by a structure or wall, making them ideal for limited spaces while still providing efficient lifting capabilities. Both types enhance material handling efficiency tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
