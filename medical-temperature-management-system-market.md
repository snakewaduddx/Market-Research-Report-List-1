<p><strong>The Medical Temperature Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Patient Warming Systems</li><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Temperature Management System Market is primarily divided into two types: Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to maintain or increase a patient's body temperature during surgeries or critical care, utilizing blankets, mattresses, and forced-air warmers. Conversely, Patient Cooling Systems serve to lower body temperature in conditions such as heat stroke or after cardiac arrest, employing cooling blankets, ice packs, and advanced devices to promote thermal regulation. Both systems are crucial for improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
