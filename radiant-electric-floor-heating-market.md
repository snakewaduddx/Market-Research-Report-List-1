<p><strong>The Radiant Electric Floor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant electric floor heating and hydronic systems are two primary types of radiant heating technologies. Electric systems use electrical cables or mats installed beneath the floor to generate heat directly, offering quick installation and responsive heating. In contrast, hydronic systems circulate warm water through pipes beneath the flooring, providing a more uniform heat distribution and energy efficiency, especially in larger areas. Both options enhance comfort and energy efficiency, making them popular choices for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
