<p><p>The plasmid transfection reagent market is categorized into liposomal-based and non-liposomal-based reagents. Liposomal-based reagents utilize lipid vesicles to enhance cellular uptake of plasmid DNA, providing high transfection efficiency and reduced toxicity. Non-liposomal reagents, on the other hand, include polymer-based and calcium phosphate methods, offering alternative mechanisms for delivering nucleic acids without liposomes. Each type presents distinct advantages, making them suitable for various applications in gene therapy, vaccine development, and molecular biology research.</p></p>
