<p><strong>The Gas Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Engine Heat Pumps (GEnHP)</li><li>Gas Absorption Heat Pumps (GAbHP)</li><li>Gas Adsorption Heat Pumps (GAdHP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas heat pumps market includes three main types: Gas Engine Heat Pumps (GEnHP), which utilize a gas engine to drive a refrigerant cycle for heating and cooling; Gas Absorption Heat Pumps (GAbHP), which use gas to generate heat that drives an absorption cycle for thermal energy; and Gas Adsorption Heat Pumps (GAdHP), which rely on adsorption processes with solid adsorbents to transfer heat. Each type offers distinct efficiencies and applications, catering to diverse energy needs in residential and industrial sectors.</p></p>
