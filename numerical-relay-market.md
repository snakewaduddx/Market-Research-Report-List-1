<p><p>Numerical relays are advanced protective devices used in electrical systems, categorized into various market types based on their application. Overcurrent protection safeguards against excessive current flows, while arc protection detects and mitigates dangerous arcs. Motor protection ensures the safe operation of motors, transformer protection focuses on preventing transformer failures, and capacitor bank protection maintains capacitor integrity. Generator protection prevents damage to generators, and the "others" category encompasses specialty protections for diverse applications, ensuring reliability and safety in electrical networks.</p></p>
