<p><p>Positive displacement compressors are categorized into several types based on their design and operation. </p><p>Reciprocating piston compressors utilize a piston to compress air or gas within a cylinder, making them efficient for various applications. Rotary screw compressors use two interlocking screws to compress air, providing continuous flow and higher efficiency for larger applications. Rotary vane compressors employ sliding vanes within a rotor, offering a compact design with versatile uses. Additionally, "Others" includes specialized designs like diaphragm and scroll compressors, catering to niche markets.</p></p>
