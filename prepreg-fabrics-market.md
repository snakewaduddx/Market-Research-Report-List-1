<p><p>The prepreg fabrics market consists of three primary types: carbon fiber, glass fiber, and aramid fiber prepregs. Carbon fiber prepregs offer high strength and lightweight properties, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Glass fiber prepregs are more affordable and provide excellent thermal and electrical insulation, commonly used in construction and electronics. Aramid fiber prepregs, known for their impact resistance and durability, are utilized in protective gear and military applications, highlighting the versatility and specialized use of each type.</p></p>
