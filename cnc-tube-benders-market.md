<p><strong>The CNC Tube Benders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC tube benders are categorized into two main types based on their power source: electric and hydraulic. Electric tube benders utilize electric motors to drive the bending process, offering precise control, reduced maintenance, and energy efficiency. They are ideal for smaller batches and tighter tolerances. In contrast, hydraulic tube benders employ hydraulic systems, providing greater bending force suitable for larger and thicker materials. While hydraulic models excel in heavy-duty applications, they generally require more maintenance and can be less energy-efficient than electric systems.</p></p>
