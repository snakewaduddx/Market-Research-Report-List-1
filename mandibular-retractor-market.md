<p><ul><li>External Fixed Traction Device</li><li>Internal Fixed Traction Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mandibular retractor market comprises two main types: external fixed traction devices and internal fixed traction devices. External fixed traction devices are applied outside the patient’s mouth, providing stabilization and positioning through external support structures. In contrast, internal fixed traction devices are surgically implanted within the oral cavity, offering direct control over mandibular alignment and movement. Both types are essential for orthodontic treatments and surgical interventions, helping to improve dental outcomes and patient comfort.</p></p>
