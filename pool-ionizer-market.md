<p><p>The pool ionizer market can be segmented into three main types: ionization, solar ion, and others. Solar ionizers harness solar energy to power the ionization process, offering an eco-friendly alternative. Other types encompass various innovative technologies or hybrid systems that combine ionization with traditional filtration methods, catering to diverse consumer preferences and environmental considerations.</p></p>
