<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Catenary Mooring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Catenary mooring systems are essential in marine applications, utilizing various anchor types. Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA) rely on resistance from seabed penetration, providing stable anchorage. Vertical Load Anchors (VLA) function by vertical loads, gripping the seabed with a combination of gravity and soil friction. Suction Anchors utilize vacuum pressure to embed themselves in the seabed, offering high holding capacity. Together, these anchor types enhance the stability and reliability of floating structures in challenging marine environments.</p></p>
