<p><p>The compressed-air motors market is categorized into three main types: vane, piston, and gear compressed-air motors. Vane motors use rotating vanes to create torque, offering smooth operation. Piston motors operate by compressing air in cylinders, providing high power output and efficiency. Gear motors utilize gears to convert compressed air into mechanical energy, ensuring precise control and durability. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, catering to varying power requirements and operational environments.</p></p>
