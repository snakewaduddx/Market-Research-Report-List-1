<p><p>The digital stethoscopes market comprises two primary types: amplifying stethoscopes and digitizing stethoscopes. Amplifying stethoscopes enhance auditory signals, allowing healthcare practitioners to hear sounds more clearly, which aids in accurate diagnoses. In contrast, digitizing stethoscopes convert acoustic sounds into digital signals, enabling sound storage, analysis, and transmission for remote consultations. Both types improve patient care by providing enhanced capabilities for detecting cardiovascular and respiratory conditions, facilitating better diagnosis and monitoring through advanced technology.</p></p>
