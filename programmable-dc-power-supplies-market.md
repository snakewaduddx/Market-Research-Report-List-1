<p><p>Programmable DC power supplies are categorized into three main types based on their output configurations. Single-output type supplies provide one fixed voltage output, ideal for simple applications requiring a specific voltage. Dual-output type supplies offer two independent outputs, allowing users to manage different voltage levels simultaneously for more complex circuits. Multiple-output type supplies can deliver various outputs with distinct voltage and current specifications, catering to intricate systems that need multiple power levels for different components, enhancing flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
