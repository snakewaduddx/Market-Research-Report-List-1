<p><p>The parallel shaft gears market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Spur gears are widely used for their simple design and efficiency in transmitting motion. Rack and pinion gears convert rotational motion to linear movement, commonly found in steering mechanisms. Herringbone gears feature a V-shaped tooth design that minimizes axial thrust, ideal for high-load applications. Helical gears provide smoother operation and are used in applications requiring quiet, efficient power transmission, making them suitable for diverse industries.</p></p>
